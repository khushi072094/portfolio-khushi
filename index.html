About Me

I am a dedicated student pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (Honors) in Data Science at Lovely Professional University. My academic journey began at JK International School, Gorakhpur, where I completed my 10th grade, followed by my 12th grade at The British School, New Delhi. These formative years laid a strong foundation for my interest in technology, problem-solving, and innovation.

From an early age, I have been deeply passionate about computer science and its limitless potential to transform lives. With a curious mind and a constant eagerness to learn, I continuously explore emerging technologies and aim to strengthen my skills in artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, data science, and the Internet of Things (IoT). I strongly believe that technology, when combined with creativity, can bring meaningful change to society.

My short-term goal is to secure a valuable role in a reputed IT company where I can apply my knowledge, gain hands-on experience, and grow both professionally and personally. I am eager to work in dynamic environments that challenge me to innovate and push beyond conventional solutions.

Looking ahead, my long-term vision is to contribute significantly to the nation and the world through impactful projects and innovative ideas, particularly in the fields of AI, IoT, and sustainable development. I aspire to leverage cutting-edge technologies to address real-world challenges, support sustainable goals, and build intelligent solutions that create a positive difference.

With persistence, creativity, and a growth mindset, I aim to transform my passion into meaningful contributions that not only advance technology but also benefit society at large.
